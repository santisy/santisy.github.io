I am Dingdong Yang (杨定东). I got my Bachelor's degree from Nanjing University, China and my Master's degree from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I did my research intern in Professor Honglak Lee's lab in Umich. After that, I did one-year full-time research assistant in Professor Thomas Serre's Lab in Brown University. I also worked as computer vision engineer in Bytedance and other companies for three years. I am now a first-year PhD student in Simon Fraser University and advised by Professor Hao (Richard) Zhang and Professor Ali Mahdavi-Amiri.

My research interests lie in generation models, especially 3D generation that closely combines Computer Graphics priors with learning based methods.
